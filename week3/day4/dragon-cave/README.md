# Dragon Cave Game

### Options
- Buy Chicken Tenders
    - Yes: Increases health by 10 cost 10 gold
    - No: Lose aura
- Fight waste yute
    - Yes: Lose 15 health, gain 5 gold, gain 25 aura
    - No: Lose 15 aura
- Buy torch
    - Yes: Torch makes it easier to fight the dragon, cost 25 gold
    - No: Nothing
- Buy sword
    - Yes: If you dont have enough aura or gold you get stabbed and robbed. The sword makes it easier to fight the dragon
    - No: Nothing
- Drink potion
    - Yes: Increases health by 15
    - No: Nothing

### Win Conditions
- Have a sword, a torch and aura
- Have more than 60 health and a torch 
- Have more than 40 health and a sword
